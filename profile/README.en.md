## 🧭 What We Do

We are dedicated to exploring the fusion paradigms and innovative possibilities of Large Language Models and Multi-Agent systems across diverse industry scenarios.

Drawing on hands-on experience in enterprise-grade backend architecture governance and cutting-edge AI research, we deconstruct complex AI pipelines and distill them into a set of **composable AI infrastructure components**. Our goal is neither to build a heavy, closed platform nor to lock ourselves into a specific vertical — but to cultivate a **more accessible, delightful, and vibrant open ecosystem for Agent development**.

Here, every capability module is a lightweight, independent component — you can adopt one to solve a specific pain point, or seamlessly assemble them into highly complex intelligent business flows.

---

## 🔬 Core Research Directions

While our concrete product matrix is still evolving, our research and engineering practices consistently focus on the following core areas:

* **Agent Orchestration**
Exploring engine-agnostic dynamic scheduling mechanisms, supporting the design of complex business workflows and standardized collaboration among multiple agents.
* **Memory & Cognition**
Breaking through the limits of traditional context windows, we research and build a long-term memory layer and self-evolution mechanisms for agents, granting AI cognitive continuity across time.
* **RAG Ecosystem**
Crafting a lightweight, embeddable knowledge co-existence architecture that allows retrieval capabilities to integrate into existing engineering systems at minimal cost.

---

## 📐 Architectural Philosophy

Regardless of how the ecosystem evolves, every piece of infrastructure we ship will strictly adhere to the following design principles:

* **Isolation** — High cohesion, low coupling. Components have clear boundaries and can be plugged, unplugged, and replaced independently.
* **Lightweight** — We ship SDKs and core libraries, not black-box monoliths. The code stays transparent and pure.
* **Embeddable** — We respect your existing stack. Our components are designed to integrate gracefully into your backend — not to force your system to adapt to AI.
* **Engine-Agnostic** — Business logic is decoupled from underlying infrastructure. Swap your preferred LLM API, vector database, or storage engine at any time, free from ecosystem lock-in.
