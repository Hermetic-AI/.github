
# Hermetic-AI

> Exploring the infinite boundaries of AI Agents — Architecture-neutral, highly composable, and elegantly simple.

---

## 🧭 What We Do

We are dedicated to exploring the integration paradigms and innovative possibilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Multi-Agent systems across diverse industry scenarios.

Drawing on practical experience in both enterprise-grade backend architecture governance and cutting-edge AI academic research, we deconstruct complex AI pipelines into a set of **composable AI infrastructures**. Our goal is not to build a bloated, closed platform, nor to tie you to specific business logic. Instead, we aim to forge a **more accessible, highly usable, and vibrant ecosystem for Agent development**.

Here, every capability module is an independent, lightweight component. You can introduce a single module to solve a specific pain point or seamlessly assemble them to orchestrate highly complex intelligent workflows.

---

## 🔬 Core Exploration Areas

While our specific product matrix is constantly evolving, our research and engineering efforts remain focused on the following core domains:

* **Agent Orchestration**
Exploring engine-agnostic dynamic scheduling mechanisms to support complex business workflow design and standardized collaboration among multiple agents.
* **Memory & Cognition**
Breaking through traditional context window limitations. We research and build long-term memory layers and self-evolving mechanisms for agents, endowing AI with cognitive continuity across time.
* **RAG Ecosystem (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**
Creating lightweight, embeddable knowledge architectures, allowing retrieval capabilities to be seamlessly integrated into existing engineering systems at minimal cost.

---

## 📐 Architectural Design Philosophy

No matter how the ecosystem evolves, all the infrastructure we produce strictly adheres to the following design principles:

* **Isolation** — High cohesion and low coupling. Clear boundaries between components ensure they are independently pluggable and replaceable at any time.
* **Lightweight** — We insist on delivering SDKs and core libraries rather than over-encapsulated black boxes, keeping the code transparent and pure.
* **Embeddable** — Respecting your existing tech stack. Our components are designed to gracefully integrate into your current backend architecture, rather than forcing your system to adapt to the AI framework.
* **Engine-Agnostic** — Decoupling business logic from underlying infrastructure. Whether you prefer a specific LLM reasoning API, vector database, or storage engine, you can switch freely without ecosystem lock-in.
